PROJECTS

KARNA
KARNA - is in collaboration with Dr. S. R. Chandrasekhar Institute of Speech and Hearing and is used to detect early deafness due to earphones status: completed
NBHS
NBHS - newborn baby hearing screening is a system that enables hospitals to keep track of the hearing milestones of newly born babies status: completed
SAA
Student assessment application is used to assess medical students on daily basis helping medical collages to have good student data storage capacity status: ongoing
VFV
Visual frequency viewer is a project that is used to help speech and hearing medical students visualize frequency in a very efficient way status: ongoing
SIS
storage inventory system is a project that is been made with Resonance for their inventory management status: ongoin
CODE-Ecosystem
